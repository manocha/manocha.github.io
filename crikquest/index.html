Starting Instructions (1/2)

  1. Make sure you've created Discord and Twitch accounts!
  2. Join the Criken Discord server by clicking here.
  3. Connect your Twitch account to Discord.
    • In Discord, go to "User Settings" (click the little gear icon next to your username at the bottom near the left) → "Connections."
    • There, connect your Twitch account by clicking on the appropriate icon and following any further instructions.
